Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 15, 2025

1. The Pact (Introduction)

The Grimoire ("we", "our", or "us") operates as a portfolio project and utility tool for Magic: The Gathering players. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data.

2. Data We Collect

  • Authentication Data: We use Supabase Authentication. We store your email address solely for the purpose of logging you in and securing your card vault.
  • User Content: We store the lists of cards (decks/collection) and preferences (theme choice, avatar) you explicitly save to the database.

3. Third-Party Services

We rely on the following oracles to function:

  • Supabase: For database and authentication hosting.
  • Scryfall API: For card images and pricing data. We do not send your personal data to Scryfall.
  • Netlify: For hosting the web application.

4. Data Deletion

You may break the pact at any time. Clicking "Break Pact" (Logout) protects your session. To request full deletion of your account and data, contact us at Westonmmj@gmail.com.
